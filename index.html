How To Play

The goal of the game is to win the big tic-tac-toe board. In order to earn one of the nine square in the big grid, you must win the smaller tic-tac-toe board contained within it.

Where you move determines which board your opponent will then have to make a move in. For example, if you choose the top right cell of the center grid, your opponent will then have to choose a cell in the top right grid.

In order to win a grid, you must either get three of your marker in a row or get five of your marker anywhere in a grid.

If your move would force your opponent to play in a grid which has already been won, your opponent is allowed to select any open cell on the whole board.
